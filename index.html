ABOUT

从结构到策略，理解完整系统。

Understanding the whole robot, from structure to policy.

我是北京航空航天大学人工智能方向博士生，就读于 4573实验室。我的机器人经历比较宽：参与过机械结构与机构设计、电气和嵌入式集成、传感器与执行器调试、机器人控制、强化学习，也在探索扩散模型和集群系统。

我不会把自己描述成每个方向都很精通。相比单点专家，我更擅长理解各模块之间的接口、约束和相互影响。大量实物搭建与调试经历，使我能够在研发团队中连接结构、电气、控制与算法，从整体视角帮助系统逐步收敛并落地。

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Beihang University, working in the 4573 Laboratory. My robotics experience is deliberately broad: mechanisms and mechanical design, electrical and embedded integration, sensing and actuation, robot control, reinforcement learning, and ongoing exploration of diffusion models and multi-robot systems.

I would not claim deep expertise in every area. My strength is understanding the interfaces, constraints and trade-offs between modules. Extensive hands-on debugging helps me connect mechanical, electrical, control and learning teams, and support system development from an overall engineering perspective.
