01 / ABOUT

Engineering intelligence into motion.把智能真正做进运动系统。

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Beihang University, working in the 4573 Laboratory. I build learning-enabled robots that must operate beyond clean simulations. My work combines reinforcement learning, model-based robot control, whole-body control, mechanical design, and embedded system integration.

我是北京航空航天大学人工智能方向博士生，就读于 4573实验室。我关注能够走出理想仿真环境、在真实系统中可靠运行的学习型机器人， 将强化学习、基于模型的机器人控制、全身控制、机械设计与嵌入式系统集成结合起来。

Robotics attracts me because equations, code, mechanisms, sensors, and hardware are forced to agree on the same machine. The gap between “works in simulation” and “works repeatedly on the robot” is the part I find most valuable.

机器人吸引我的地方在于：公式、代码、机构、传感器和硬件必须在同一台机器上同时成立。 从“仿真里能跑”到“真机上可重复运行”的差距，是我最感兴趣也最愿意投入的部分。

My longer-term goal is to study generalizable robot learning and hybrid model-based / learning-based control across different morphologies — from humanoids and quadrupeds to aerial and reconfigurable robots.

下一阶段，我希望研究更具泛化能力的机器人学习方法，以及模型控制与学习控制的融合， 让策略能够跨越不同形态，从人形、四足扩展到飞行机器人和可重构机器人。

02 / RESEARCH

Research focus研究方向

Robot Control & Reinforcement Learning机器人控制与强化学习

Learning-based control, policy optimization, robust deployment, sim-to-real transfer, and the integration of model knowledge with data-driven policies.

学习控制、策略优化、鲁棒部署、仿真到现实迁移，以及模型知识与数据驱动策略的融合。

Whole-Body & Legged Robotics全身控制与足式机器人

Coordinated locomotion and manipulation for humanoids, quadrupeds, and quadruped manipulators, with an emphasis on whole-body behavior.

面向人形、四足和四足机械臂系统的运动—操作协同，重点关注全身行为与控制。

Aerial Robotics & UAV Systems飞行机器人与无人机系统

Autonomous UAV platforms, flight control, onboard AI computing, LiDAR integration, long-range communication, and anti-UAV system design.

自主无人机平台、飞行控制、机载AI计算、激光雷达集成、远距离通信与反无人机系统设计。

03 / PROJECTS

Selected engineering work代表性工程项目

P-01
RL · WBC · SIM2REAL

Quadruped Manipulator: RL + Whole-Body Control 四足机械臂：强化学习与全身控制

A simulation-to-real workflow for quadruped locomotion and manipulation, using reinforcement learning together with whole-body control. The system is developed and compared across Isaac Lab, MuJoCo, and a real robot platform.

面向四足机器人运动与操作协同的仿真到现实流程，将强化学习与全身控制结合， 并在 Isaac Lab、MuJoCo 与真实机器人平台之间进行开发和验证。

Reinforcement Learning Whole-Body Control Isaac Lab MuJoCo Sim-to-Real
MECHANISM · RECONFIGURATION

Serial–Parallel Hybrid Reconfigurable Robot 串并混联可重构机器人

Mechanical and system design of a robot that changes its configuration through a serial–parallel hybrid architecture. The project emphasizes mechanism synthesis, reconfiguration, actuation layout, and practical prototype implementation.

采用串并混联架构实现构型变化的机器人机械与系统设计，重点包括机构综合、 重构方式、驱动布局与实体样机实现。

Serial–Parallel Mechanism Reconfigurable Robot Mechanical Design Prototype

P-03
UAV · AUTONOMY · MECHATRONICS

Autonomous UAV Platforms & Anti-UAV Systems 自主无人机平台与反无人机系统

Design and integration of multiple UAV platforms, including a four-camera + LiDAR platform, a dual-axis gimbal + LiDAR platform, a net-capture anti-UAV system, and a signal-suppression soft-kill platform. Work covers structural design, propulsion, flight control, onboard computing, sensing, power, and communication.

设计并集成多款无人机平台，包括四目相机+激光雷达平台、双轴吊舱+激光雷达平台、 网捕式反无人机系统以及信号压制软杀伤平台。工作覆盖结构、动力、飞控、机载计算、 传感器、电源与通信链路。

UAV Design Flight Control LiDAR Orin NX Mesh Communication System Integration
P-04
HARDWARE · EMBEDDED AI

Compact Aerial Robot System Engineering 紧凑型飞行机器人系统工程

A compact aerial robot integrating flight control, propulsion, omnidirectional sensing, onboard edge AI, LiDAR-based autonomous navigation, and long-range communication. The project focuses on mass, power, thermal, wiring, and mechanical packaging constraints.

在紧凑平台中集成飞行控制、动力系统、全向感知、机载边缘AI、激光雷达自主导航 与远距离通信，重点解决重量、供电、散热、布线和机械布局等系统约束。

Edge AI Autonomous Navigation Power Architecture Mechanical Packaging Embedded Integration
