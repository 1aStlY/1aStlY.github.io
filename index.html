ABOUT

把智能做进真实系统。

Engineering intelligence into real systems.

我是北京航空航天大学人工智能方向博士生，就读于 4573实验室。我关注能够离开理想仿真、在真实系统中可靠运行的学习型机器人，将强化学习、基于模型的机器人控制、全身控制、机械设计与嵌入式系统集成结合起来。

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Beihang University, working in the 4573 Laboratory. I build learning-enabled robots that must operate beyond clean simulations by combining reinforcement learning, model-based control, whole-body control, mechanical design and embedded integration.
